Delivery & Shipping

How we get your parts to you — quickly and safely

1. UK Delivery Coverage

We deliver nationwide across the United Kingdom. Whether you're in Birmingham, London, Manchester, Edinburgh, or anywhere in between — we can get your parts to you.

  • ✓ Mainland UK
  • ✓ Northern Ireland
  • ✓ Scottish Highlands
  • ✓ Isle of Man & Channel Islands (additional charges apply)

2. Delivery Times

  • Small Items (lighting, electronics, interior parts): 1-3 working days
  • Medium Items (bumpers, doors, body panels): 1-3 working days
  • Large Items (engines, gearboxes, complete axles): 2-5 working days (pallet delivery)

* Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on your location.

3. Delivery Costs

Delivery costs are calculated based on:

  • Item size & weight - Small parts ship via courier, large parts via pallet
  • Your location - Mainland UK vs. remote areas

* Contact us for an exact delivery quote. We always aim to offer the most competitive shipping rates.

4. Large-Item Shipping (Pallet Delivery)

For engines, gearboxes, and other heavy items, we use trusted pallet delivery services.

  • ✓ Secure, palletised shipping
  • ✓ Tracked delivery
  • ✓ Requires a forklift or tail-lift for unloading
  • ✓ Delivery to a business address preferred
