About Cherrywood Auto Parts

Birmingham's trusted source for quality used VAG parts

Who We Are

Cherrywood Auto Parts is a family-run vehicle recycler based in Birmingham, West Midlands. We specialise in breaking Audi, Volkswagen, SEAT, and Škoda vehicles – giving you access to quality used parts at affordable prices.

With years of experience in the motor trade, we understand the importance of finding the right part quickly. Our yard has 1,000s of tested components ready to go, from engines and gearboxes to doors and interior trim.

What We Do

  • Vehicle Breaking - We buy and strip complete vehicles for parts
  • Used Parts Sales - Components tested and ready for collection or delivery
  • Scrap Collections - We buy accident-damaged and non-running VAG models
  • Nationwide Delivery - Fast courier shipping across the UK
