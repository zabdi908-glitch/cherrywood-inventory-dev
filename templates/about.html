About Cherrywood Auto Parts

Birmingham's trusted source for quality used VAG parts

Who We Are

We're Cherrywood Auto Parts — a family-run vehicle breaker based in Bordesley Green, Birmingham, specialising in Audi, Volkswagen, SEAT and Škoda. Every vehicle that comes through our yard is stripped and checked by hand, so what reaches you is a genuine used part that's been tested, not just pulled off a shelf.

Whether you're after an engine, gearbox, body panel or interior trim, our stock is updated regularly and backed by a 90-day warranty on all mechanical parts. Can't see what you need? Message us on WhatsApp with your reg or part number and we'll check the yard for you — most enquiries get a same-day answer.

What We Do

  • Vehicle Breaking - We buy and strip complete vehicles for parts
  • Used Parts Sales - Components tested and ready for collection or delivery
  • Scrap Collections - We buy accident-damaged and non-running VAG models
  • Nationwide Delivery - Fast courier shipping across the UK
