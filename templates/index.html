Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver nationwide across the UK?

Yes. We arrange fast tracked courier shipping for smaller loose items and secure commercial heavy pallet delivery options for engines, transmissions, and heavy door panels.

Do you buy scrap cars?

Yes. We buy accident damaged, faulty, or MOT failure Audi, VW, SEAT, and Škoda models for breaking. Contact our team with details for an instant quote pay-out.

Are your parts tested?

Yes. All parts are fully tested before being listed. We provide a 90-day warranty on all mechanical components.
