Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver nationwide across the UK?

Yes. We arrange fast tracked courier shipping for smaller loose items and secure commercial heavy pallet delivery options for engines, transmissions, and heavy door panels.

Do you buy scrap cars?

Yes. We buy accident damaged, faulty, or MOT failure Audi, VW, SEAT, and Škoda models for breaking. Contact our team with details for an instant quote pay-out.

Can I collect parts directly from the yard?

Yes, on-site collections are welcome from our yard collection counters based out of Birmingham. We recommend contacting us on WhatsApp first to ensure your component is unbolted and ready.

Are your used auto parts tested?

Where physically possible, yes. Electrical items, alternators, motors, and mechanical components are thoroughly inspected and verified running before being added into clean storage shelving.
