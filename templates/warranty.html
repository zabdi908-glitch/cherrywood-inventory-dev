Warranty Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Our Warranty Promise

At Cherrywood Auto Parts, we stand behind every part we sell. All used parts come with a 90-day warranty covering mechanical failure. This warranty ensures you can buy with confidence, knowing that your part has been tested and is ready to perform.

2. What's Covered

  • Engine Components - Complete engines, cylinder heads, and internal components
  • Transmission & Drivetrain - Gearboxes, differentials, and drive shafts
  • Electrical Parts - ECUs, alternators, starters, and sensors
  • Body & Trim - Doors, bumpers, lights, and mirrors
  • Suspension & Brakes - Calipers, discs, and suspension components

*If the part fails due to a manufacturing defect within 90 days, we will replace it or refund your money.

3. What's Not Covered

  • Wear & Tear - Brake pads, clutch plates, and other consumable items
  • Misuse - Damage caused by improper installation or racing
  • Modifications - Parts modified after purchase
  • Cosmetic Damage - Scratches, dents, or paint issues existing before sale
  • Accidental Damage - Damage from accidents or negligence

4. How to Claim

If you believe a part is faulty, follow these steps:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 90 days of purchase
  2. Provide proof - Order number, part description, and photos of the issue
  3. Return the part - We'll arrange collection or guide you on return
  4. We'll resolve - We'll inspect and offer a replacement or refund

5. Fitting & Compatibility

We recommend professional installation for all mechanical parts. We are not liable for:

  • Incorrect fitting or installation
  • Compatibility issues (we provide details to help you verify)
  • Damage caused during installation
